# 9-Signal Platform Formation Operating Model

## Summary
Operationalize the “9 Signals” as a scoring and execution system that identifies where government ambition, corporate readiness, and capital interest converge without commitment architecture—then converts that pressure into artifacts that unlock governance, risk allocation, and capital deployment.

## Observations
- The 9-signal framework reliably captures pre-deal platform pressure.
- Signal overlap is most valuable when paired with explicit friction diagnosis.
- Without an architecture response, high-signal environments remain narrative-heavy and non-committal.

## Assumptions
- We are targeting stage 2 -> stage 3 transitions (Pressure -> Architecture).
- We prioritize opportunities where commitment constraints are design problems, not demand problems.
- Every validated signal cluster must produce an execution artifact.

## Scoring Model (0–2 per signal, max 18)
For each signal:
- 0 = absent
- 1 = emerging/partial
- 2 = clear and corroborated

Signals:
1) Government ambition without structure
2) Corporate tech readiness signaling
3) Capital circling without deployment
4) Multi-power-center outcome convergence
5) Repeated narrative at forums/panels
6) Policy papers without implementation
7) No actor willing to move first
8) Early projects stalling/failing
9) Language shift from project -> system scale

## Action Thresholds
- **0–7 (Monitor):** Track only; no heavy build.
- **8–12 (Shape):** Build architecture hypothesis + stakeholder map.
- **13–18 (Originator):** Launch full platform-design play (governance/capital stack/sequencing).

## Friction Map (required companion)
For any score >=8, map 3 frictions:
1. Governance friction (who decides, how decisions clear)
2. Risk allocation friction (who absorbs what risk at each phase)
3. First-mover friction (why all actors wait)

## Required Artifacts by Threshold
### 8–12 (Shape)
- Platform Hypothesis Memo (1 page)
- Stakeholder/Power-Center Map (1 page)

### 13–18 (Originator)
- Governance Blueprint
- Capital Stack Draft (equity/debt/ECA/DFI pathways)
- Sequencing Plan (90-day, 180-day, pre-FID milestones)
- Counterparty Outreach Script (role-specific)
