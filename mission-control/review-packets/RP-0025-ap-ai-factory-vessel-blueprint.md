# Andhra Pradesh AI Factory — Vessel Blueprint (Pre-FID)

## Summary
This platform is in a high-pressure pre-FID state: sovereign enablement + EPC backbone + hyperscaler anchor exist, but commitment architecture is missing. The immediate objective is to define the vessel (governance, capital stack, risk allocation, and sequencing) before investor outreach.

## Commitment Architecture (1-page draft)

### 1) Governance Spine
- **PlatformCo (HoldCo):** owns strategy, governance, capital formation.
- **ProjectCo(s):** phased campus SPVs (MW-to-GW expansion sequence).
- **Decision rights:**
  - Government: land/power/policy compliance gates
  - EPC (L&T): schedule, budget, execution covenants
  - Tech anchor (Nvidia): technical standards/interoperability + defined reserved matters per equity rights
  - Sponsor/GP: capital sequencing + investor governance + downside protocols

### 2) Capital Stack Sequencing
- **Anchor equity:** Nvidia (reported 15%) + Sponsor/GP layer (to be finalized)
- **LP equity:** sovereign/infra/strategic capital after governance package lock
- **Debt layer:** project/infrastructure debt only after minimum bankability conditions met
- **Contingency capital:** construction-delay and power-availability reserve structures

### 3) Risk Allocation Matrix (minimum)
- Construction risk -> EPC performance framework + completion security
- Power risk -> enforceable supply terms + fallback provisions
- Offtake/tenancy risk -> minimum contracted demand thresholds
- FX/political risk -> hedging + policy protections where required
- Governance failure risk -> deadlock mechanisms + escalation protocol

### 4) Bankability Minimum Commitments (before broad LP/debt outreach)
1. Enforceable land and concession terms
2. Power terms with reliability/price clarity
3. EPC terms with milestones + remedies
4. Tenancy/offtake structure with minimum commitment logic
5. Governance charter (reserved matters, veto boundaries)
6. Data-room package index with owner/accountability

## Outreach Script Templates (Anchor Invitation)

### A) Anchor Candidate Script (Sayyu-style)
"We are not offering a generic ticket. We are defining an anchor position in a pre-FID platform where governance rights, downside protections, and commitment sequence are being set now. We’d like to discuss a defined anchor role tied to sponsor architecture, not passive LP exposure."

### B) Sovereign/Infra LP Script
"This is a structured pre-FID entry with defined governance and risk-allocation milestones. We are sequencing sponsor and anchor commitments first, then opening LP participation against a bankability package rather than narrative-only exposure."

### C) Strategic Operator Script
"Your role is not capital-only; it is standards and utilization credibility inside a governed platform model. We’re structuring commitment terms that preserve execution velocity while keeping risk ownership explicit."
