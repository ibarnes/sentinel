# UAE Refined Products Corridor Platform — Vessel Blueprint (Pre-FID)

## Summary
A UAE-based platform coordinating Dangote refinery export volumes with global buyers through structured governance, capital sponsorship, and logistics distribution.

## Governance Spine
- **HoldCo model:** Singapore HoldCo coordinating capital participation and governance alignment.
- **ProjectCo model:** UAE DMCC OpCo managing corridor trading, logistics coordination, and distribution.
- **Decision rights:**
  - Sponsor/GP: TAFF
  - LP investors: Strategic trading houses, commodity investors, family offices

## Capital Stack Sequencing
- **Anchor equity:** TAFF family capital
- **Sponsor/GP equity:** Dangote refinery supply participation
- **LP equity:** Commodity trading houses and institutional investors
- **Debt layer:** Trade finance lines from international banks
- **Contingency capital:** Inventory and cargo financing facilities

## Risk Allocation
- Construction risk owner: Dangote Group
- Power risk owner: Dangote Refinery
- Offtake risk owner: Corridor SPV with contracted buyers
- FX risk owner: Trade finance banks
- Political risk owner: Multilateral insurance providers

## Bankability Minimum Commitments
1. Committed refinery export allocation
2. Anchor buyer participation
3. Distribution partner alignment
4. Trade finance facility approval
5. UAE corridor operating license

## Outreach Sequence Rule
Structure first, then outreach.

## Anchor Invitation Script
“We are establishing a UAE-based distribution corridor for Dangote refinery exports. Anchor participation provides priority offtake access and governance input into the platform architecture.”
