# Workflow A — Buyer/Pressure Monitor
Schedule: Daily 06:30 ET
Output: Morning Brief section — "Pressure Surface Changes"

## Goal
Wake up to "who moved + why it matters" across shortlist buyers.

## Initial Shortlist
- GIC
- PIF
- Brookfield
- GIP

## Inputs
- Trusted internal notes and approved research sources.
- Any Isaac-provided watchlist updates.

## Process
1. Collect new signals (allocations, mandates, infra announcements).
2. Normalize by buyer and date.
3. Score signal strength: High / Medium / Low.
4. Draft implications for pipeline pressure/opportunity.
5. Package in Morning Brief format.

## Output Contract
- Observations
- Assumptions
- Recommendations
- Next actions for Isaac to approve

## Ready for Review Criteria
- At least one validated signal check per shortlist entity (or explicit "No material change").
- Clear "why it matters" statement per significant move.
